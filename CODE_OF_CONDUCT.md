# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want the `terminal-bridge-v2` community to be open, practical, and respectful for users building with AI tooling across different platforms and workflows.

Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to make participation harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- showing empathy and patience
- giving actionable technical feedback
- assuming good intent while being clear about risks or regressions
- respecting differing experience levels and tool choices
- focusing criticism on code, docs, and behavior, not people

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- personal attacks or insulting language
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- deliberate disruption of community discussions
- bad-faith reporting or review abuse

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, issues, commits, code, wiki edits, discussions, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

For general conduct concerns, open a private maintainer contact through GitHub or use the support and security channels described in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) and [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.
